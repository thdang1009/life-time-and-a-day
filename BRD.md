# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Title: "Lifetime & A Day" (Project 1314)

**Version:** 1.0

**Prepared by:** Gemini (Senior BA)

**Stakeholders:** You (The Romantic Architect) & Your Wife (The Muse)

---

### 1. Project Overview & Vision

The goal is to create a digital sanctuary that commemorates the **1,314 days** of love between the two Stakeholders. The site will serve as a living timeline, starting from the spark of first love (**July 10, 2022**) to the sacred union (**December 24-25, 2024**) and beyond.

**The "Why":** To transform 1,314 days from a number into a narrative, ensuring the "Forever" promise is documented in the digital ether.

---

### 2. Strategic Objectives

* **Zero-Cost Sustainability:** $0/month maintenance using static hosting.
* **Emotional Resonance:** High-impact visuals and micro-interactions (floating hearts, fading text).
* **Milestone Highlighting:** Special focus on the wedding transition (from "Dating" to "Married").

---

### 3. Target Audience

* **Primary:** Your wife (The Surprise Factor).
* **Secondary:** Future versions of yourselves looking back on these early years.

---

### 4. Functional Requirements (The "Love" Specs)

| ID | Feature | Description | Emotional Value |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **FR-01** | **The 1314 Counter** | A dynamic counter showing days, hours, and minutes since July 10, 2022. | Real-time proof that "Every second counts." |
| **FR-02** | **"Chapter Two" Marker** | A special visual transition for Dec 24, 2024. The background changes from "Warm Sunset" (dating) to "Pure White/Gold" (marriage). | Validates the growth of the relationship. |
| **FR-03** | **Interactive Timeline** | A scrollable path featuring key dates (First date, First trip, "I Do"). | A walk down memory lane. |
| **FR-04** | **Secret Love Note** | A password-protected or "click-to-reveal" section containing a personal letter. | Adds an element of intimacy and mystery. |
| **FR-05** | **Music Backdrop** | Auto-play (if browser allows) or a play button for "Your Song." | Sets the mood instantly. |

---

### 5. Non-Functional Requirements (The Technical Soul)

* **Accessibility:** Must be mobile-responsive (so she can view it on her phone while cuddling).
* **Performance:** Ultra-lightweight (loading in under 2 seconds) to ensure the magic isn't lost to a loading spinner.
* **Infrastructure:** Hosted on **GitHub Pages** or **Vercel** (Free Tier).
* **Domain:** A free sub-domain (e.g., `our1314.vercel.app`) or a free `.tk/.ml` if available.

---

### 6. Content Map (The Storyline)

1. **The Landing Page:** A large "1314" in elegant serif font. A subtitle: *"Since July 10, 2022. From the first 'Hello' to 'I Do'."*
2. **The Wedding Gallery:** A grid of photos from your ceremony on Dec 24-25, 2024, with a "Just Married" badge.
3. **The Promise:** A closing quote: *"1314 days down, a lifetime to go."*

---

* **Phase 2:** Hardcoding the dates ().

* **Phase 4:** The Big Reveal on Day 1314.
